Betting Strategy

Play Blind to keep stakes low, or See your cards but pay double to stay in!

HOW TO PLAY:

1. All players place an Ante (Boot amount) to start.
2. Players are dealt 3 cards face down.
3. On your turn, choose to Call/Raise (Blind) or See your cards.
4. "Seen" players must bet double the amount of "Blind" players.
5. You can Fold at any time to exit the round.
6. A Sideshow can be requested if only 2 players remain.
7. The best hand wins the Pot at Showdown!
